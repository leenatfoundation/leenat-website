About LeEnat

LeEnat is a nonprofit initiative dedicated to supporting rural school girls in Ethiopia through menstrual hygiene assistance, education support, and community empowerment.

💜 Our Vision

Every girl deserves equal access to education and dignity.

🎯 Our Goal

Reduce school absenteeism by providing menstrual hygiene products.

🤝 Community

Working together with volunteers and donors to change lives.

Our Mission

We believe that no girl should miss school because of her period. Through donations and partnerships, LeEnat provides sanitary pads, education, and hope.
